# The $KYRT Governance Manifesto — Strategy & Sovereignty

**Strategy Speaks Integrity**

## I. The mandate

The Kerythos AI ecosystem is professionally architected business infrastructure, not a decentralized experiment. $KYRT is governed under a sovereign leadership structure where every strategic move is guided by **Integrity, Precision, and Scalability**.

## II. Custody & security

The treasury and the token's core authorities are protected by institutional-grade controls:

- **Hardware-bound authorization** — treasury movements require physical, cryptographic validation through isolated hardware modules; no transaction executes via software alone.
- **Multi-signature consensus** — strategic actions such as buybacks, burns, and infrastructure upgrades require the synchronized validation of multiple independent keys. This eliminates any single point of failure.

## III. The integrity of the fair launch

$KYRT is committed to transparent distribution:

- **No insider access** — no presale, no team or investor allocation, no hidden mint authority.
- **Community Rewards** — a Rewards pool is reserved at genesis to fund *Refer & Earn*. It is distributed to users through real product usage, **never sold**.
- **Immutable supply** — once the supply is deployed, the mint authority is permanently revoked. Scarcity is guaranteed by the code itself.

## IV. Revenue-driven deflation

Governance extends to the economic model. A portion of the ecosystem's revenue funds the **Buyback & Burn** protocol:

- **Market repurchase** — revenue is used to buy $KYRT directly on the open market on Solana.
- **Algorithmic scarcity** — repurchased tokens are split **50% permanent burn** / **50% Expansion Vault**, so the token's trajectory reflects the ecosystem's operational success.

Exact parameters are maintained in [`docs/TOKENOMICS.md`](docs/TOKENOMICS.md).

## V. The path forward

We do not seek external contributions to the core architecture. Our strength lies in closed-loop technical excellence and the physical security of our assets. We invite the market to participate in the growth of the Kerythos ecosystem, built on a foundation of unyielding integrity.

**Strategy Speaks Integrity.**
